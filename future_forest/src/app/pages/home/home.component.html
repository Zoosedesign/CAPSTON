Our Mission

for the right purpose

We believe that tossing 100 seeds into a field doesn't mean planting 100 trees. And we think that heavily planting a single species isn't a sustainable and biodiversity-friendly choice. Trees bring benefits to you, your surroundings, and the entire planet. We believe each of us should make an effort to cut down our CO2 emissions, and in doing so, we can also help absorb them by planting trees. We reckon we can make this planet greener, and we're determined to do it right. Plus, we only plant drought-resistant plants, reducing CO2 emissions while responsibly preserving water usage.

The right tree

in the right place

Planting trees to make them grow, with a long-term perspective. Planting trees is complex work. Not only from a technical point of view skills and resources are needed to create suitable places for small trees to germinate and grow but also, and above all, from a strategic point of view. Planting trees is not enough, they must be supported in their growth. This means thinking in the medium and long term, and for this you need to find the right tree for the right place and the right purpose. The projects we carry out for the planting of our trees respond exactly to these needs.


Who we are?

One Family, Many Faces

We are the ones who love nature and strive to make a positive change in the places we call home, not just for ourselves but for others as well. We envision a world where the concrete jungles of today are transformed into vibrant, green spaces. We understand the importance of bringing nature back into the urban landscape, revitalizing the areas where it has been stripped away. However, we also recognize the need to tread carefully, ensuring that we don't squander other invaluable resources. Water, in particular, holds a special place in our hearts, for without it, life simply cannot exist. Therefore, as we embark on this mission to restore nature's presence, we do so with a keen awareness of the need to conserve and protect the precious resources that sustain us all. Together, let's create a future where lush greenery thrives amidst the gray, and where sustainability and reverence for our natural world go hand in hand.

We are nature lovers who aim to improve our living spaces for ourselves and others. Our mission is to bring back greenery to our cities, while being mindful of conserving important resources like water. We strive for a sustainable future where urban landscapes thrive with lush greenery. Together, we can make a difference by nurturing our environment and creating a greener tomorrow.
